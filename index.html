Longhorn Data Exchange

We improve student organizations at UT Austin by delivering insights, options, and reviews to leaders so that they can make well-informed decisions.

Running a student organization at UT is hard, as leaders often must make important decisions with little information. We help student organizations by providing them with the information they need to make the right decisions for their situation.

The information we provide comes from student organizations here at UT Austin, which we believe provide the best insights into current trends, options, and resources. We actively seek the best experiences from organizations and curate it into useful information which everyone can use. We hope that you find our information helpful and consider contributing your own organization's experiences.

Single Upload

Single upload is for single entries of information. You'll see a form to our database which will allow you to enter information. Please note that if you have lots of data to contriubte, you may find the bulk upload system much more convenient.

Activity Exchanges

Contribute data relating to activities, such as socials, volunteer opportunities, speakers, companies, and food vendors.

Coordiantion Exchanges

Contribute data about upcoming major events open to the public and major projects you want the public to know about.

Borrow Exchanges

Contribute items which can be borrowed by other organizations. This includes information about the item and the expectations about usage.

Technology Exchanges

Contribute information about what technology services you use and how you use them.

Training Exchanges

Contribute training material, either documents, slideshows, or links, which you think others may find helpful in any capacity.

Attendance Upload

We currently don't have an exchange regarding attendance data, but we are still collecting it for future use.

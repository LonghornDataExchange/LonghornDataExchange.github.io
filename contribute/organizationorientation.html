Organization Orientation

Welcome!

This page acts as a short explanation of why, how, and what we expect from you. We hope that this helps address questions you may have and smooths the upload process. If you have additional questions, we encourage you to email us [email coming soon].

Introduction

What Problem Are We Solving?

For most officers in most organizations, there is a point in the planning process where the officer must determine what options are available. For a social chair, this might be what socials to do for the next semester. For a food-buyer, this might be which restaurants to cater from. At this point, the officer must reasearch new options or consult a list of past actions.

We want to be that list, built by everyone, for everyone.

But more than just past events, we want to build databases which are usable and helpful in sharing options, opportunities, and future plans among organizations.

What We Do

We host curated lists which we call exchanges.

We think that student organizations make UT a special place by providing opportunities to students to learn, grow, and lead, and therefore active and operationally healthy organizations improve the school.

We gather information from other student organizations around campus.

Running an organization, even with good ideas and good students, requires a lot of effort and determination. Furthermore, student organization leaders are often faced with many problems which may be beyond the "expertise" of the organization. We believe that many of these problems have already been solved by some other organization on campus, and that by sharing these "solutions," organizations will run better.

We analyze, enrich, and aggregate the inforamtion into views that you can use.

Central to our vision is a collaberative attitude among you, ourselves, and other organizations. We know that you know how to do things, and we want to share this knowhow with our fellow students here at UT.

The Process

Step 1: You send data to us.

We need data from you to start. You can submit information either by single upload or bulk upload. 

Step 2: We post and enrich the data.

We take the information you send us and mostly post it. Part of this posting includes us adding enriching and aggregating the information to make it useful to everyone. 

Step 3: You see what others are doing.

Once the information is online, you are free to browse what organizations are doing by going to our visit page.

Some Details

Everything is voluntary.

We don't demand that you give us any specific or exhaustive list of information. If you don't want to share somethign, we won't bother you to share it.

Expect that what you tell us will become public knowledge.

This is a public webiste, so please expect that the information you give to us will be seen by the public, including UT administration.

This is a work in progress.

We're still setting up new systems and opening new exchanges. Please bear with us and this product will get better and better!

Socials

The social exchange is designed for officers who need to plan social events. We ask that you contribute:

You contribute:

  • Activities
  • Locations
  • Approximate Price per Person for each Dvent
  • Estimated Popularity of each Events
  • Estimated Quality of each Events

We provide a list of:

  • Activities
  • Locations where that activity happened
  • Approximate Price per Person
  • Average Estimated Popularity of the Event
  • Average Estimated Quality of the Event

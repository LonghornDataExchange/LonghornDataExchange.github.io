Documents

Find the applicable exchange and complete the single upload form or the bulk upload form.

Exchange Purpose Role Single Upload Bulk Upload Form
Banquets Assist in the preparation, planning, and foresight that needs to go into organizing a banquet. Banquet planner(s) Link to Banquet Form Banquet
Caterers Provide a scaled list of caterers for different events. Officer(s) that order food for events and meetings Link to Caterer Form Caterers
Corporate Supporter Provide names and information of corporate supporters. Corporate relations officer(s) Link to Corporate Supporter Form Corporate Supporters
Equipment Allow for organizations to borrow/rent equipment, that would otherwise be more difficult to obtain, through the LDE equipment database and equipment contracts. More information on the contract types are available on the Equipment Exchange document. Note: When using this exchange, only list equipment you organization personally owns, not items that your organization has access to. President, operations vice president, secretary, or any other applicable position(s) Link to Equipment Form Equipment
Officer Structure Provide insight so organizations can function efficiently and effectively to meet their goals and needs for their specific missions. Senior leadership officers Link to Officer Structure Form Officer Structures
Social Provide locations, information, and ideas for social events. Social or events coordinator(s) Link to Social Form Socials
Speakers, Tours, and Workshops Provide names and various information about speakers for events, including panels, workshops, fireside chats, tours, etc. Academic and corporate relation officer(s) Link to Speaker Form Speakers
Technology Provide information on popular technologies and services used by organizations (internally and externally). This includes but is not limited to: officer communication, member communication, sales, inventory, etc. Common examples include: Slack, Facebook Messenger, GroupMe, Snapchat, Instagram, Facebook, Websites, Google Drive, etc. Senior leadership officers Link to Technology Form Technology
Volunteer Opportunities Provide locations, information, and ideas for volunteering events. Volunteer and outreach organizer(s) Link to Volunteering Form Volunteering
